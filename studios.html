Meet The Team

M.r Veng Thavong

Deployment and Maintenance

Angkearith is a Certified Brand Strategist, designer, and design lecturer. Since 2012, he has been working with clients in industries such as Chavon hospitality, CADT, Forte, and PlerngKob. He utilizes design and strategy to create meaningful changes. He is passionate about building and empowering the next generations of designers. Occasionally, he organizes workshops and talks related to design and creative business. Angkearith helps bridge the gap between strategy and creativity.

M.r Sen Makara

Coding and Testing

Both is an award-winning designer with a reputation for strong and timeless design solutions, with an emphasis on concept and strategy. He led the notable identity redesign of Baramey which helped revitalize the company's image as being one of the leading music labels for original music in Cambodia. M.s Phorn Pisey

UX/UI Designer

Visal is a classically trained illustrator and designer, having graduated from the Royal University of Fine Arts in Visual Communication. In his eight-year career, he has built experience within multiple art mediums such as acrylic painting, pencil work, watercolor painting, and recently, digital art. His work often explores the intersection between art and design and even crosses into the physical like the trophy design for the Cambodian Premier League, the top football league in Cambodia. Visal brings a uniquely mature perspective to the team.

M.r Chin Dalik

UX/UI Designer

Sambath is a multidisciplinary designer, illustrator, and artist with a keen interest in experimental typography. With a bachelor's in computer science and various leadership experiences in other fields, his ability to adapt to unfamiliar situations and disciplines is unrivaled. Outside design, he often explores environmental advocacy, having joined programs like YSEALI and SSEAYP. His works often utilize color and mixed media and have been featured by ATypl, and Sa Sa Art Projects.

M.r Sek Seyha

Planning and Requirenents Analysis

Sovichet is currently one of the most influential type designers in Cambodia, specializing in Khmer script. He started type design as a hobby in 2010 and turned professional in 2018. Sovichet has published both open-source and bespoke typefaces for international and local corporations such as Google, Grab, Carlsberg Group, and Peng Huoth Group. Besides designing typefaces, he writes about Khmer type design and typography and conducts workshops and talks in the Cambodian design community.
