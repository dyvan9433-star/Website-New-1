While brand identity design in Cambodia has improved significantly in the past decade, there tends to be a disconnect between typography and identity design, in which typography is often overlooked compared to other visual elements within a brand's tool kit. In this talk at BITS10 in Bangkok, our Type Director Sovichet Tep and Creative Director Both Bou discuss the current landscape of Cambodia’s up-and-coming design industry and explore the common problems facing brand identity design relating to the lack of education and resources around Khmer typography and type design. We hope our inclusion in this event will help foster a stronger connection between Watch the talk here our country and the rest of the world.
